Welcome to Mandarin 8, located in the Mandarin Oriental Hotel in Las Vegas, Nevada. Join us as we take you on a journey throughout the different regions of China presented by Michelin-starred chef, Kevin Leung. Kevin grew up in Guangzhou, China and has spent years learning under some of the highest-regarded chefs throughout China. It is an honor to have you as our guest. Come, let's eat.

At Mandarin 8, we offer a balance between traditional and modern Chinese cuisine that encompasses the different regions all across China. From spicy, bold Szechuan influences, to the fresh ingredients in Cantonese cuisine, we're here to take you on a journey to the far east.
